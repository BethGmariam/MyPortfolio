About Me

My name is Beth Gmariam. I am energetic, entusiastic to learn new things, specially technology. My passion toward Mathematics and Physics lead me to study Electrical Engineering. After I graduated from Elecrical Engineering, I worked for Engineering consulting firm in Oakland California. In 2013, while working in the company, I took Engineering licensing exam (EIT), I passed the exam with my first try.

In 2015, I joined another Engineering firm in San Francisco. This new company utilizes 2D/3D modeling softwares (Revit, AutoCAD,SKM...etc). My responsibility, as Electrical Designer, was to design and model electrical system layout on those 2D/3D modeling computer softwares.

In 2018, I started taking a fullstack web development program at University of Toronto (UofT). I have completed the following programing languges and tools, with hands on experience working on projects:

HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, JSON, Firebase, Node.JS, Constractors, Object Oriented Programming Concepts, mySQL, etc.
